Sunday home session — 30 min total
1 · 3 min
World's greatest stretch
5 reps each side. Hip flexor, thoracic, shoulder all in one flow.
2 · 3 min
90/90 hip stretch
60s each side. Internal + external hip rotation — reduces lateral load stress.
3 · 3 min
Pigeon pose or figure-4
60s each side. Deep glute and hip — directly offloads lower back from court play.
4 · 2 min
Half-kneeling hip flexor stretch with overhead reach
60s each side. Counters hip flexor tightness from court stance and sitting.
5 · 2 min
Child's pose to thread-the-needle
45s each side. Thoracic rotation — decompresses spine after a week of swinging.
6 · 2 min
Supine spinal twist
60s each side. Lower back and thoracic decompression.
7 · 2 min
Wall ankle mobility drill
10 reps each side. Dorsiflexion — affects lunge depth and court footwork.
8 · 2 min
Standing quad + couch stretch
45s each side. Quad and hip flexor lengthening.
9 · 5 min
Box breathing (4-4-4-4)
Inhale 4s · Hold 4s · Exhale 4s · Hold 4s. Lying flat. Activates parasympathetic recovery.
10 · 5 min
Match visualization
Eyes closed. Replay a recent point sequence — your movement, your resets, your shot selection. Then visualize executing perfectly in a tough scenario.
Phase progression: extend holds to 75s (P2), 90s (P3). Visualization — P1: movement patterns · P2: match scenarios · P3: pressure situations
Shoulder prehab warm-up — Day B (5 min before lifting)
1
Band pull-aparts — 2×20
Rear delt and rotator cuff activation. Critical before any pressing.
2
Band external rotation — 2×15 each side
Anchor band at elbow height, rotate outward. Best rotator cuff prehab for racket sport athletes.
3
Wrist flexor + extensor curls — 2×15 each
Light dumbbell. Prevents forearm tendinopathy from repetitive pickleball swing load.
4
Arm circles — 10 forward + 10 backward
Full shoulder capsule warm-up to close out prehab.
Agility drills — Wednesday/Saturday (8–10 min before drilling)
Phase 1
Lateral shuffle cone drill + agility ladder in/out steps
4 cones 10 ft apart. Shuffle touch each cone × 5 sets. Then 2 ladder lengths of in/out. Low stance, quick plant.
Phase 2
T-drill + ladder crossover steps
Sprint forward, shuffle left, shuffle right, backpedal. 4–5 reps, 90s rest.
Phase 3
Reactive partner drill + full T-drill circuit
Partner points left/right/forward at random — react instantly for 20s × 5 sets.
